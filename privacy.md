# Privacy Policy for ImmaShap

**Last Updated: May 11, 2026**

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application **ImmaShap** ("Application") for mobile devices. The Application is an educational tool focused on South African biomes.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
**ImmaShap does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or sensitive user data.**

*   **Personal & Sensitive Data:** We do not collect, use, or share data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, or device identifiers.
*   **Permissions:** Any permissions requested by the Application (e.g., Notifications) are used solely for real-time app functionality and do not result in data collection or transmission to our servers.

## 2. Data Security and Encryption
Because the Application does not collect or transmit user data off the device, there is no data in transit to encrypt. However, we follow industry best practices to ensure that any data stored locally on your device remains within the Application's private sandbox, protected by the Android operating system's security features.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The Application does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data-sharing SDKs. No data is shared with third parties for any purpose.

## 4. Account Deletion and Data Retention
ImmaShap does not use a cloud-based account system. 
*   **Account Deletion:** Since no accounts are created, there is no account data to delete. 
*   **Data Deletion:** Users have full control over their local data. You may delete all app-related information at any time by clearing the Application's cache or uninstalling the Application from your device.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
Our Application is intended for an audience of all ages, including children. Because we do not collect any personal information, we are in full compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and international privacy standards.

## 6. South African Users (POPIA Compliance)
In accordance with the **Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)**, we confirm that we do not process, share, or store the personal information of our users. As a South African developer, I respect the constitutional right to privacy and ensure this Application remains a "privacy-first" experience.

## 7. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
