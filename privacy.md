# Privacy Policy for ImmaShap

**Last Updated: May 11, 2026**

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application **ImmaShap** ("Application"). ImmaShap is a wellbeing and safety utility designed to act as a silent safety net by sending automated alerts if a user misses a scheduled check-in.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
To provide its core safety features, the Application requests and processes the following information:

* **User Information:** Name, used to personalize the safety alert.
* **Emergency Contact Information:** Name and Email address of a designated third party.

**Purpose of Collection:** This data is used strictly for the Application's core functionality: the "Emergency Check-in" system. If a user fails to respond to scheduled reminders, the Application uses these details to send a warning email to the designated contact.

## 2. Data Storage and Transmission
* **Local Storage:** All names and email addresses entered into the Application are stored locally within the Application's private sandbox on your device.
* **Transmission (Backend):** When an emergency alert is triggered, the Application transmits the contact's email address and the user's name to our service providers (**Firebase** and **Brevo**) solely for the purpose of delivering the email.
* **No Permanent Server Storage:** We do not maintain a permanent database of your emergency contacts on our servers. The data is processed ephemerally to facilitate the alert.

## 3. Data Security and Encryption
We take the security of your information seriously:
* **In-Transit:** All data transmitted between the Application, Firebase, and Brevo is protected using **HTTPS/TLS encryption** to prevent unauthorized interception.
* **On-Device:** Data is protected by the Android operating system’s security architecture.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Advertising
* **Service Providers:** We share data with Firebase (Google) and Brevo strictly for infrastructure and email delivery services.
* **Advertising:** This Application is supported by ads. These third-party ad networks may use the Google Advertising ID to serve relevant ads. You can manage these permissions in your Android device settings.
* **No Data Selling:** We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or emergency contact information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 5. Account Deletion and Data Retention
ImmaShap does not require user accounts.
* **Control:** You can modify or delete your contact information at any time within the "Settings" menu.
* **Full Deletion:** You can permanently delete all data by clearing the Application's cache or uninstalling the Application. This removes all local records and halts any future transmissions.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
ImmaShap is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

## 7. South African Users (POPIA Compliance)
In accordance with the **Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)**, we ensure that:
* We only process data necessary for the safety service you have requested.
* Users are responsible for obtaining consent from their emergency contacts before entering their details into the Application.
* We maintain the integrity and confidentiality of the information processed.

## 8. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
